THE VALLEY OF FLOWERS, Uttarakhand

Welcome to The Valley of Flowers!

About

Bestowed with the rare and exotic Himalayan flora, the Valley of Flowers is a bouquet of nature peacefully snuggled in the West Himalayan region of Uttarakhand. The trek of Valley of flowers goes through dense forests, along with Pushpawati river and can be reached by crossing many bridges, glaciers and waterfalls on the way. This glaring piece of nature is an Indian National Park and a UNESCO World Heritage Site exhibiting plush meadows blossomed with endemic alpine flowers and diverse fauna in this valley.

Why we love Valley of Flowers?

The region of value of flowers is rich in varied flora and fauna. The value of flowers was declared As national Park of India in 1982 and is now UNESCO World Heritage site. The virgin beauty of this mystical Valley Has always lured botanists, nature lovers and adventure lovers. Tucked at 3,658 Metres in Bhyundar valley , The value of flowers is in Chamoli district sprawls Over a past expanse of 87.50 kmÂ² measuring 8 km long and 2 km wide. Value of flowers is the second core zone of the Nanda Devi biosphere reserve blessed with numerous cascading waterfalls slivery glaciers and thicket that greet the visitant at every step. Ghangaria, Tiny hamlet situated 13 Kilometre from Gobind Ghat is the last human habitation centre on the valley of flowers.

Fun Fact

The value of flowers seems to change it colours throughout the season from May to October,as blossoms hundreds of different flowers ,taking on various colours and shades as time progresses. The valley is home to many celebrate your flowers like the Brahma Kamal, The blue Poppy and the cobra lily. It is a much sought destination for the flower lovers botanist, trekkers, Photographers, bird watchers and wildlife photographers. The valley is very popular among international tourist and thousands of foreigners flock to Valley of flowers every year The locals always knew about the existence of the valley and believe that it was inhabited by fairies and gods. It was also believed earlier that one goes to the valley is captured by fairies and never returned back.
